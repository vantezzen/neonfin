## What vantezzen/pay owns

vantezzen/pay owns the small app-facing layer:

- wallets and recovery codes
- balances and deductions
- feature access derived from purchases, subscriptions, and manual grants
- provider checkout creation
- webhook fulfillment
- drop-in React components

Stripe or Polar still own money movement, invoices, payment methods,
subscription billing, cancellation screens, and provider-side refunds.

<Callout>
The safest pattern is to send customers to the provider portal for billing
changes, and let provider webhooks update vantezzen/pay after payment, cancellation,
renewal, or refund events.
</Callout>
