vantezzen/pay has a small vocabulary:

- **Project**: the app you are monetizing.
- **Product**: the thing users spend, like images, minutes, exports, or
  requests.
- **Credits**: metered units a wallet can spend.
- **Feature**: a named capability unlocked by a price, subscription, or manual grant.
- **Price**: a package users can buy.
- **Wallet**: where one user's credits live.
- **Credit code**: an anonymous wallet recovery code.
- **Provider**: Stripe or Polar, used for checkout, invoices, and payment
  methods.

You do not need to know the internal schema to use vantezzen/pay. You only need to
pick the right identity mode, create clear products and prices, and deduct
credits at the right time.
